OWL MAN IS HERE TO WRECK THINGS UP.

- The end of the level is long inescable pit.
- Attacking bullets will reflect them back the direction from whence they came.

Controls
Keyboard
	Arrow Keys: Move
	C: Attack
	X + Left/Right: Dash
	Down + X in air: Dive Kick
	Space: Jump
	Down on ground: Pose
Controller
	DPad: Move
	Left Stick: Move
	Left trigger: attack
	Right trigger + direction: Dash
	Down + right trigger in air: Dive Kick
	Bottom Button: Jump
	Down on ground: Pose
